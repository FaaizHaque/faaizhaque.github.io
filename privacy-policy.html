Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025  |  Applies to: NPAT (Name Place Animal Thing)

1. Information We Collect

NPAT is a multiplayer word game. To enable multiplayer features we collect:

  • A display name you choose when creating a game session
  • Game session data (answers, scores, round results)
  • Device push notification token (if you grant permission, for game invites)

We do not collect your real name, email address, or any account credentials.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID on Android, or IDFA on iOS) to serve personalised or contextual ads. On iOS, we request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission before any personalised ad data is collected.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit policies.google.com/privacy.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate real-time multiplayer game sessions
  • Send game invite notifications (with your permission)
  • Display relevant advertisements

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Game session data is shared only with other players in the same session. Ad data is handled by Google AdMob under their own privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

Game session data is retained for 30 days, then automatically deleted. Notification tokens are removed when you uninstall the app or revoke notification permission.

6. Your Rights

You may opt out of personalised advertising at any time via your device settings (Limit Ad Tracking on iOS / Opt out of Ads Personalisation on Android). To request deletion of any data associated with your sessions, contact us.

7. Children

NPAT is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, open an issue on the app's store listing or contact us via GitHub: github.com/FaaizHaque.
