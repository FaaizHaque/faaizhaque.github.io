Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?

Your single-player progress is saved automatically on your device. Make sure you don’t delete the app to keep your level progress and stars.

Why isn’t my answer being accepted?

Answers must start with the chosen letter and be a valid entry in the selected category. Try a different spelling or a more common version of the word.

How does the Daily Challenge work?

A new challenge is available every day with 6 random categories. You get one attempt per day — complete it as fast as possible to climb the global leaderboard.

How do I play multiplayer?

Tap Multiplayer on the main menu, then either create a game and share the code with friends, or enter a code to join an existing game. Supports 2–10 players.

What are stars used for?

Stars are earned by completing levels and through milestone bonuses (every 100 points = 2 bonus stars). You can spend stars to unlock hints during gameplay.

I lost my progress after reinstalling — can I get it back?

Unfortunately single-player progress is stored locally on your device and cannot be recovered after deletion. We’re working on cloud backup for a future update.
